My Business Story

Chocolate that preserves precious family moments—less sweet, more deep.

It began simply with a love for chocolate, which led me to leave for Paris.
The air of Paris, unfamiliar alleyways, and the classes I encountered at Le Cordon Bleu.
What I learned there was not just recipes.
It was how a single bite can change a person's mood, and on some days, even hold onto a memory.

As time passed, having a child in Australia and living through the COVID era made it even clearer.
Family moments are shorter than we think, and our hearts tend to be postponed.
Feelings left unspoken pass easily,
but taste remains long after, often recalling the expressions and atmosphere of that day.

That is why Verygood decided not to make it just sweet.
Rather than pushing strong sweetness, we chose to leave the depth of cacao,
so that the lingering finish after one bite stays longer.
We crafted a texture where the nuttiness of nuts and the acidity of fruits—the first impressions of the ingredients—start vividly and finish cleanly.

Since a gift is also a way to convey one’s heart,
we captured the understated beauty and warm sentiment of Korea in our package.
A neat atmosphere, like Minhwa (folk painting) and Bojagi (wrapping cloth), without being excessive,
and completed minimally using recycled paper.
We adhere to basics with HACCP-certified processes and inspect directly to maintain stable quality.

We make chocolate, but in truth, we craft moments.

When you think of chocolate, Think Verygood.
